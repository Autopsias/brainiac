## What this controls

Whether the nightly chief-of-staff composes reply drafts into your Drafts
folder for threads that warrant a response. It NEVER sends — sending is yours,
structurally.

- `enabled: true` (or this file absent) — compose drafts, cap 10/run.
- `enabled: false` — no drafts; the run spends that budget on battlecards.

## Why this file exists

Without it a run has nowhere to read your answer, so a low draft-engagement
count invites it to quietly stop drafting and ask you again every night. Your
ruling lives here instead: durable, and outside the rolling skill-memory cap.

Engagement (did you send/edit a draft?) is still measured and reported. It is
DATA, not a verdict — an untouched draft may simply mean you have not looked.
